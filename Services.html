Services

SHY Aviation specialise in exclusive charters worldwide – be it jet, helicopter or yacht. We tailor itineraries to your
exact preferences, from business trips to family holidays. Our goal is to deliver an experience that goes beyond bespoke.

Our carefully selected global partnerships grant us access to every type of aircraft. We can locate the correct jet or
helicopter for your needs, ensuring you and your loved ones arrive safely and hassle-free.

A single text, email or call is all that is required to start the booking process. Tell us where, who with and where you wish
to fly, and we will present the available options for you to chose from.

SHY accepts all major credit cards, debit cards and ask transfers. All funds must be cleared prior to take-off.
