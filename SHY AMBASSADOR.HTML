AD HOC CHARTER

An ad hoc charter flight is an unscheduled flight that is not part of a regular airline routing. With a charter flight, you rent the entire aircraft and can determine departure/arrival locations and times.

HELICOPTER SERVICES

Helicopter services are ideal for hotel, ski resort and event transfer. Our network of brokers can ensure priority landing slots, meaning our clients will always arrive on time and in style.

GROUP CHARTER

Group charter involves moving large numbers of passengers from A to B. Our team have the expertise and experience to handle all sorts of complex personnel movements.

CARGO CHARTER

Cargo charter is a large part of our business. We help to move freight, from aviation parts to medical equipment, across the globe in major disasters or time critical missions

LIFESTYLE SERVICES

Our lifestyle services vary from complex adventure travel, to events & ticketing and personal shopping. We are able to resource premier packages to highly-acclaimed international happenings.

What’s my earning potential?

AD HOC CHARTER, HELICOPTER SERVICES, GROUP & CARGO CHARTER

You will receive 50% of the commission earnt on each specific flight after the operations overhead charges of 10% have
been paid.

JET CARD

You will receive commission on each Jet Card purchased to the value of 5% of the total value of the card after the
operations overhead charges of 10% have been paid.

Ambassador commission is uncapped, and your earning capacity is only limited to your ability make successful introductions to SHY Aviation.
In addition to commissions received from flights, our Ambassadors also have the ability to earn through the Ambassador-2-Ambassador
Referral Scheme and through any additional revenue generated for SHY Lifestyle from client introductions.
The Ambassador Program does not apply to existing clients of SHY Aviation or SHY Lifestyle.
